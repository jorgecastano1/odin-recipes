Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • Lentils (1 lb)
  • Carrots
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • White Onion
  • Yellow Potatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Water or Chicken Broth (12-16 cups)
  • Spices:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
    • Paprika
    • Cayenne
    • Cumin

Instructions

  1. Chop Green Bell Pepper, White Onion
  2. Chop Carrots and Potatoes
  3. Separate stems from Cilantro and finely chop stems, save leaves for garnish
  4. In a Large Pot, stir fry Cilantro Stems, Green Bell Pepper, and White Onion for 8-10 minutes
  5. Season with one teaspoon of each spice
  6. After 8 minutes add washed Lentils and Carrots and stir fry for 4-5 more minutes
  7. Add Water/Chicken Broth and one teaspoon of each spice again
  8. Cook for 30 minutes and then add peeled and diced Yellow Potatoes
  9. Cook for an additional 15-20 minutes
  10. Last 5 minutes of cooking add finely chopped Cilantro
  11. Serve over White Rice, garnish with Cilantro leaves, and enjoy!
