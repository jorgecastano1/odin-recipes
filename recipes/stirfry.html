Chicken Chow Mein

Ingredients

  1. Egg Noodles
  2. Chicken Breast/Thigh
  3. Green Onion
  4. Carrots
  5. Mushroom
  6. Cabbage
  7. Optional for velveting: Flour
  8. Sauce Ingredients:
    • Soy Sauce
    • Cooking Wine
    • Hoisin Sauce
    • Oyster Sauce
    • Sesame Oil
    • Optional: Gochujang/Chili Crisp

Instructions

  1. Boil Noodles according to packaging and set aside
  2. Combine 3 teaspoons of Soy Sauce and one teaspoon of all other sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix
  3. Thinly slice chicken and place in a bowl
    • If Velveting, add one teaspoon of flour
  4. Add one teaspoon of soy sauce and cooking wine to chicken and mix
  5. Separate whites from Green Onions and finely chop, save green parts for garnish
  6. In a large pot, cook chicken until sllightly charred, set aside
  7. In the same pot, add chopped green onion, carrot, and cabbage, stir fry for 8 minutes
  8. Add chopped mushroom and stir fry until mushrooms are done (4-5 minutes)
    • Add water if pot dries out
  9. Add chicken, noodles, and sauce to pot. Mix and simmer together for about 3 minutes
  10. Garnish with chopped green onions, chili crisp, and enjoy!
