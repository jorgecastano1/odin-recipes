Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeño
  • Limes
  • White Vinegar
  • Spices:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
    • Cumin

Instructions

  1. Finely chop tomatoes, red onion, jalapeño, and cilantro
  2. Combine chopped vegetables into a large bowl and season with one teaspoon of each spice
  3. Juice the limes and use a splash of white wine vinegar for added acidity
  4. Cover bowl and let repose in fridge for about 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
